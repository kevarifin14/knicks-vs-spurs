The dynasty's spine

Two big men, three guards, one coach.

David Robinson
"The Admiral." MVP, naval officer, and the foundation the first title was built on.

"The Big Fundamental." Five titles, two MVPs, 19 seasons — the greatest power forward ever.

The moments that became legend

Every dynasty has its myth.

New York · 1970
"Here comes Willis"
Reed, barely able to walk, hit the Knicks' first two baskets of Game 7. The Garden erupted; Frazier did the rest. The most famous entrance in NBA history.
San Antonio · 2014
The beautiful game
After blowing a title to Miami in 2013, the Spurs came back and dismantled the Heat with the prettiest passing basketball the league had seen. Redemption, on their terms.
Two banners hang at the Garden. Five hang in San Antonio.
